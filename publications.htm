Publications by Stephen Taylor include:

  • Schijnbare terugkeer en nieuwe idealen (on organ music and organ playing in the Netherlands 1940- 1960), in: Orgels van de Wederopbouw, Walburg Pers, Zutphen, 2006.
  • Het Marcussen-orgel in de Nicolaïkerk in Utrecht, in: Orgels van de Wederopbouw, Walburg Pers, Zutphen, 2006.
  • Who Created the Present Organ in St Bavo's? (co-author Frits Elshout), in: The Haarlem Essays, Dr J. Butz Musikverlag, Bonn, 2014.
  • The Danish Connection – organs by Marcussen & Søn in the Netherlands (1953-1973), in: The Organ Yearbook 2008 and 2009, Laaber Verlag, Laaber.
  • Present-day organ-building problems and their practical solution. Sybrand Zachariassen's lecture revisited (co-author Paul Peeters), in: The Organ Yearbook 2015, Laaber Verlag, Laaber.

CD recordings by Stephen Taylor include:

  • 50-jaar  Sweelinck-orgel  (Marcussen  1953):  works  by  Reda,  Heiller,  David,  Pepping,  Bornefeld, Micheelsen, Distler and Gagnebin. Issued by the author in 2003.
  • 50-jaar Marcussen-orgel Nicolaïkerk Utrecht: with co-organist Ko Zwanenburg, works by a.o. Walcha, Sweelinck, J.S. Bach and Distler. Issued by the Nicolaïkerk, Utrecht, 2006.
